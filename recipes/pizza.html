Pizza

Description

This classic pizza recipe features a golden, crispy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for a cozy dinner at home or a fun meal with friends, this pizza combines simple ingredients to create bold, satisfying flavors.

The dough is easy to prepare and can be customized to your preferred thickness, while the sauce can be made fresh or from store-bought ingredients. Whether you enjoy traditional Margherita, pepperoni, or veggie-loaded pizza, this recipe provides a delicious base for endless variations and a great way to practice your cooking skills.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (7g) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare the dough: combine yeast, sugar, and warm water, let it sit for 5 minutes, then mix with flour, salt, and olive oil until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise for 30–60 minutes until doubled in size.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  5. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your chosen toppings.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve.
